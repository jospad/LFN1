Find out more about LFN

Little Feet Nursery is a friendly and happy place for children to spend their early learning years.The place is carefully chosen and designed to provide warmth and safety.

The nursery is staffed with qualified international teachers. Classes are small in size to maximize the child's attention.

We follow the 'Early Childhood Curriculum' and the 'Montessori method'.

These systems are child centered, activity based and provides all around development of the child during the early years from 2 to 4 years.
